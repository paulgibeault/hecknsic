How to Play

Objective: Rotate clusters of 3 hexagons to create matches of 3 or more same-colored pieces.

Controls

  • Select: Click or Tap a cluster.
  • Rotate:
    • On-screen arrow buttons
    • Q / E keys
    • Left / Right arrow keys

Special Pieces

★ Starflower: Form a ring of 6 same-colored tiles. Rotates like a dial (one step).
Y Black Pearl: Form a ring of 6 Starflowers. Rotate in a Y-shape. Matches clear everything.
💣 Bomb: Clear before the timer runs out!
